My Story

I’m an architect - turned - UI/UX designer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I have 4+ years of experience communicating through design, collaborating with various stakeholders and understanding key scenarios that the humans I am designing for will encounter.

My background in architectural design has helped me develop an eye for detail and taught me that innovation is inevitable in order to thrive. As a designer, I am passionate about creating seamless user experiences though aesthetics and problem solving.

Beyond work, I love to travel and discover new places. I love to leave my comfort zone and try out new adventure sports which help me improve my fitness level and gain new skills. As much as I love going out, I love to spend time with my family at home, binge watch Netflix (currently obsessed with Dark) and play board games.
