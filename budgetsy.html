budgetsy

About the project

Overview

To help people save money quickly for a big purchase and manage ther finances. Making saving money a priority is never fun, but it can be made easier thanks to budgetsy. The 5 W’s are questions whose answers are considered basic in information gathering or problem solving.

Timeline

3 Weeks

Role

User Research, User Interface Design, Visual Design, Branding

The Challenge

Saving money can be really hard, especially when you don't have a long time to do so. It is also hard if you are new to the professional world. The challenge is to design a tool for people who want to save money quickly for a reason.

The Solution

A native app allowing all data on income and expenses to be recorded easily, on the go, and from a variety of devices. Users will utilize the tool in the period before a planned expense and view accurate information on their finances.

Identifying the ideal user

Understanding the user

I created different user persona’s to understand the different scenarios why the targeted age group will use the app. This gave me a better understanding on why people want to use digital tools to help them manage their finances.

Security is key

Entering a 4 - digit numeric password or using Face ID to access your profile everytime you sign in.

Seamless onboarding

When the application is first downloaded and a user creates an account, the onboarding screens serve as a guide for new users. This helps users familiarize with the core features and functionality of the application.

Financial overview

The Home screen provides an overview of your account information, saving goals and budget. It is clean, minimal and displays important information in a well organized and competent way.

Creating saving goals

You need to input some basic information to create a saving goal. You can view a short summaty of your most recennt saving goals on the home screeen or a detailed summary on the saving goals page.

Statistics and Transactions

The app makes use of graphs and piecharts to provide you with an overview all your financial data. Wherever important, we also provide details of your finanical information.
