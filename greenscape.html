Greenscape

About the project

The Challenge

People might not have the resources to hire a landscape designer to help them design the outdoor spaces in their homes. They need help creating layouts, selecting plants and outdoor furniture to transform the space.

The Solution

To design an application which can easily connect landscape designers with potential clients. With the help of a designer’s expertise, users can easily give their patio or backyard a makeover!

Context

People love to sit and sip a cup of their morning coffee in their backyard or patio. A well designed outdoor space can contribute significantly to our well-being and quality of life. Now that so many companies are moving to the concept of permanently working from home, these outdoor spaces at your home will be even more important to create a healthier atmosphere and have a positive impact on your life. There a multiple apps in the market which help people with decorating interior of their homes.

There isn’t an app in the market which specifically for landscape design services. Who wouldn't love to transform their mundane patio space and give it a makeover? Choosing the right plants and outdoor furniture can be a hard task especially for people who don’t have any gardening knowledge. Despite working as an architectural and interior designer for the last 4 years, I find it difficult to decorate the patio in my apartment.

An online landscape consultant can help users pick the right plants based on their location and needs. They can also help in designing the space by curating a set of plants, outdoor furniture and outdoor accessories.

User Archetypes

Identifying user needs

User research was important as this is a new concept. I wanted to reach out to users from different age groups to identify their needs and requirements. I conducted user interviews with 5 users located in US and India. I focused on asking them questions about the importance of outdoor space.

What I learnt

A picture is worth a thousand words

As designers it is important to showcase our work to clients through graphic representation. Adding well photographed images, pictorial descriptions can help users take decisions easily.

Simpler is better

When there aren’t too many similar existing apps in the marketplace, users can find it difficult to navigate through in the app. A nice clean and minimal layout can helps users get accustomed to the app easily. Chosing the right style guide and branding is also important to crearte a connect with your users.As designers it is important to showcase our work to clients through graphic representation. Adding well photographed images, pictorial descriptions can help users take decisions easily.

Documentation is important

Documentation helps to ensure consent and expectations. It helps to tell the narrative for decisions made, and how the team or the users responded to different situations.

Next Steps

Moving forward, there are various additional features which can be explored in the later stages.

  1. Inbuilt shopping experience which can help users easily order the selected items and get them delivered to their doorstep.
  2. Allow users to interact with a designer if they need any minor suggestions in the future.
  3. Develop the plant care feature so that users can get reminders when to water a particular plant.
  4. Ability to add additional plants to the list so that users can have a database of all their plants in one locations.
